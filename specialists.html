Waxing Only

Waxing is a method of hair removal that involves applying a layer of wax to the skin, allowing it to harden, and then quickly pulling it off, removing the hair from the root. It's a popular choice for both men and women to achieve smooth skin, commonly done on areas like legs, arms, bikini line, and face. The process can be a bit uncomfortable but results in longer-lasting smoothness compared to shaving. Additionally, frequent waxing often leads to thinner and finer regrowth over time. Overall, waxing offers a relatively efficient and effective way to maintain hair-free skin.
